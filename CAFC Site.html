The Caribbean Association of Fire Chiefs unites emergency services professionals across the Caribbean to advance integrated emergency management, share best practices, and strengthen community safety. Through collaboration and professional excellence, we build a safer Caribbean for all.

What We Stand For

Leadership

Cultivating the next generation of fire service leaders across the Caribbean through mentorship, training, and shared governance.

Excellence

Setting and upholding the highest standards in emergency response, professional conduct, and community service throughout the region.

Regional Cooperation

Building cross-border partnerships that allow Caribbean nations to respond collectively to crises and share vital resources.
